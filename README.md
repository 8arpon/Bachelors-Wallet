# 💸 Bachelor's Wallet - Student Expense & Debt Tracker
**A 100% AI-Generated Android Application for Smart Financial Management.**

Bachelor's Wallet is a smart financial companion designed for students and bachelors living in messes or hostels. It helps manage daily expenses, track debts with friends, and follows a strict **80/20 budgeting rule** to ensure financial stability throughout the month.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 📊 Smart Budgeting (The 80/20 Rule)
* **Automated Allocation:** Automatically splits income into **80% for Food** and **20% for Others**.
* **Safe Daily Spend:** Dynamically calculates a "Safe Daily Spend" limit based on remaining days in the month.
* **Security Lock:** Protects budget configurations with a passcode-protected editing mode.

### 🤝 Advanced Debt Manager (IOU)
* **Borrow/Lend Tracker:** Visualizes "I Owe" and "They Owe" statuses with progress bars.
* **Quick Payment:** Mark transactions as "Paid" with a single tap to update balances instantly.

### 📑 History & Analytics
* **Category Breakdown:** View spending history filtered by Daily List or Category Wise charts.
* **PDF Export:** Generate and download professional monthly reports directly from the app.

### 🎨 Premium UI/UX
* **Material 3:** Modern design built entirely with Jetpack Compose.
* **Dual Theme:** Full support for both Light and Dark modes.
* **Daily Reminders:** Custom notifications to ensure consistent expense logging.

* **Language:** Kotlin

* **Architecture:** State-driven UI with a centralized DataManager
* **Notifications:** AlarmManager & BroadcastReceiver
